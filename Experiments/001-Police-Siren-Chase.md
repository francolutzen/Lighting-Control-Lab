# Experiment 001 — Police Siren Chase

## Objective

Create a police siren effect using:

* 2 LM30A Mini Spider 8×3W RGBW
* 2 RGBW PAR fixtures
* DMX192 Controller

The objective is to explore how a simple color palette can create strong real-world associations.

## Inspiration

The experiment is inspired by emergency vehicle warning lights.

Reference Image:

IMG_Reference_Police_Siren

## Equipment

Fixture 1 → RGBW PAR

Fixture 2 → RGBW PAR

Fixture 3 → LM30A Mini Spider

Fixture 4 → LM30A Mini Spider

## Programming Concept

Red and blue colors alternate rapidly.

The PAR fixtures provide large-area color washes.

The Mini Spiders provide movement and directional energy.

Together they simulate the visual behavior of emergency vehicle lighting.

## Demonstration

police-siren-demo.gif

## Observations

Even with only four fixtures, the red-blue alternation immediately creates a recognizable police association.

The moving beams generated by the Mini Spiders increase realism and visual intensity.
