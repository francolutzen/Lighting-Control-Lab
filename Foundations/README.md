# Foundations

Foundations contains the core concepts that support every other area of Lightning-Control-Lab.

Before creating atmospheres, programming shows, synchronizing lighting with music, documenting projects or experimenting with new ideas, it is important to understand the underlying principles of DMX lighting systems.

The documents in this section focus on the fundamental building blocks of lighting control.

## Purpose

The goal of Foundations is to develop a clear understanding of how lighting systems operate at a technical level.

Rather than memorizing procedures, the objective is to understand the concepts that make lighting control possible.

This knowledge becomes the foundation for all future work in:

* Lighting Control
* Show Design
* Atmosphere Creation
* Music Synchronization
* Experimental Programming
* Live Operation
